Symbolisms

Colors — There are three colors used to signify different aspirations. Gold signifies brilliance and distinction in the academic and civic fields. Red, symbolizes unwavering courage to search and fight for what is right. Black, the symbol for infinity, Stands for everlasting spirit of brotherhood

Crown — The symbol for supremacy, guides its members on their role as dynamic and effective leaders of the school and the community they are a part of.

Laurel Leaves — The leaves represent the seven founders, its thirty chartered members and the fraternity's first adviser.

Knot — It represents the common principles and ideals that unite and bind its members.

The Thinker — It symbolizes the "Rightful thinking" expected of its members.

RHO TAU GAMMA — This complements “The Thinker" which expresses the fraternity's philosophy: R stands for Right; T for Truth; and G for God. As an Organization, RTG stands for Right Thinkers Group.

Cogitare Est Vivere — Translated as: "To Think is to Live ". It is the fraternity's motto.

Manuel S. Enverga University — The name of the school where RTG saw its birth.

Lucena City — The place where the school is located.

1960 — The year the fraternity was founded.

First Chartered Fraternity — The recognition given by the school as an official registered organization.

The Greek letters P T Γ — Stands for Right Thinkers Group.
